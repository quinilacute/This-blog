Melanin

Melanin is produced by cells called melanocytes, which are found in the epidermis, the outermost layer of the skin.
melanocytes produce two types of melanin,namely
  • Eumelanin, this give the skin a brown or black color,
  • Pheomelanin, which gives the skin a red or yellow color.
